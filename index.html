My Life

It´s football time!

Yeah as you know I like football very much.. I play football since I was 3 years old. I started in Dresden and played there for 11 years. As you can imagine, it was kind of hard to combine football with school. Normally I had at least three football sessions plus a game in a week. Since I was ten I had five football sessions a week plus game. I went do a special sport school in Dresden, which is focused to comine professional sport with the duty of school. With 14 I moved to Dortmund and played for Borussia Dortmund. At my Time in Dortmund I made memories I do not want to miss my whole life! In the U15 I played for the German national team and had a tournament in the Caribik. The following years I travelled a lot due to the tournements and international games. The best trips were to Namibia and South Korea but also the games in the Youth League, so I saw most of the big cities in Europe. In the U23 I got injured and had to quit with professional football. Australia

See you Down Under!

After my bad injury I wanted do get my mind clear. A fresh start in my new life! My Choice to travel around Australia a time was one of the best decisions in my life so far! I saw so many beautiful places (I can not count every beach I saw) and met a lot new friends there. I bought a big car and started to travel around the south and east coast. This two pictures of me are showing two of my favourite places:

  • Phillip Island: What an island! The best thing here a the smallest pinguins of the world! In the evening you can see them coming out of the water to go back to there families. It is definitely worth a visit here. Come with your car and you can drive around the whole island in one day and see a lot of things. Not only the pinguins, especially the Koala Park is great! Here you can see Koalas in there natural habitat: On eucalyptus trees!
  • Melbourne: I. Love. This. City. You want to eat amazing food? Come to Melbourne. You want to enjoy the nightlife? Come to Melbourne. You want to see great architecture? Come to Melbourne. This city has its own vibe and is one of the most enjoyable and relaxed city I was. Everybody is friendly and kind, you can go to the train station here and you can have five new friends in ten minutes if you want to. What else can I say about this city... Melbourne? One day I am coming back to you!
